About Us

Welcome to rewire, a non-profit dedicated to promoting mental wellness and reducing stigma. Founded in 2022, we provide accessible resources, advocacy, and education. Our mission is to ensure everyone has access to quality mental health care, regardless of background. With passionate professionals and volunteers, we raise awareness, offer support services, and advocate for mental health parity. Whether you're seeking help or support for a loved one, we're here for you. Join us in creating a community where mental health is valued and supported. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those affected by mental illness.
